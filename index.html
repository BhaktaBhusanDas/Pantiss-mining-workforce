Pantiss Employability Training Services

Shaping Lives, Empowering communities

Sewing Machine Operator

A 3 month residential course providing employment assurance to youth with little no educational qualification in the garment export industry across Bangalore, Chennai & Tirupur. Typically made free of cost for the trainees through govt. schemes like Aajeevika, OSEMS, OSFDC etc., Pantiss trains & places over 2,000 operators per annum and employs them with companies like Shahi Exports, Raymond, Texport, Aquarelle etc.
